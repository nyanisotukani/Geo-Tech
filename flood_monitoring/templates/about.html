GeoTech: Flood Monitoring and Early Warning System

About Us

GeoTech is committed to addressing the growing challenge of flooding in Africa through innovative, real-time flood monitoring and early warning systems. Our mission is to provide actionable insights that help communities, policymakers, and emergency responders better prepare for and respond to flood events, ultimately enhancing climate resilience and reducing the impact of disasters.

Our Objectives

Real-time visualization of flood-prone areas to help communities understand their risk and take precautionary measures.
Integration of Digital Earth Africa (DEA) datasets to deliver accurate flood predictions, aiding policymakers in crafting effective disaster management strategies.
Interactive maps that allow users to select regions and view detailed data layers, supporting emergency responders in planning and executing timely interventions.
Alerts and notifications for extreme weather events to ensure timely evacuation and disaster preparedness for at-risk populations.

How GeoTech Works

GeoTech utilizes advanced technologies to provide real-time data and insights. Our backend is powered by Django, while Leaflet is used for interactive map features. The DEA API is integrated to ensure accurate and up-to-date flood data. This combination enables us to offer a comprehensive and user-friendly platform for monitoring flood risks and managing disaster response effectively.

Impact and Benefits

Our solution significantly improves community resilience by offering timely and accurate flood information. It supports policymakers with essential data for informed decision-making and helps NGOs and emergency responders plan and execute their response strategies more effectively. By addressing the challenges of flood management, GeoTech plays a crucial role in reducing the impact of disasters and enhancing overall climate resilience.
